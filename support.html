Frequently Asked Questions

What is VeriShot Location Proof?

VeriShot Location Proof allows you to capture photos that include verification metadata such as timestamp and location coordinates. These proof records are stored on your device and can be viewed, shared, or managed by you.

Where is my data stored?

All photos and verification data are stored locally on your device unless you choose to share them using iOS sharing features.

We do not upload your photos or proof data to our own servers.

Why does the app request location access?

Location access allows the app to attach latitude and longitude data to a photo at the moment it is captured, helping establish proof of where a photo was taken.

You can enable or disable location access at any time in iOS Settings.

How do subscriptions work?

VeriShot offers optional subscriptions that unlock higher verification limits and additional features.

  • Payments are handled securely by Apple via the App Store.
  • Subscription status is managed through your Apple ID.
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

How do I restore a previous purchase?

If you’ve already subscribed and reinstalled the app or switched devices:

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to the Upgrade tab
  3. Tap Restore Purchases

What happens if I reach the free verification limit?

Free users are limited to a set number of verifications per month. Once the limit is reached, you can upgrade to continue using the app without interruption.

How do I delete a photo or proof record?

You can delete proof records directly from within the app. Deleting the app from your device will also remove all locally stored app data. Photos saved to your device’s Photos app remain until you delete them.

The app is not working correctly. What should I do?

Please try the following:

  1. Make sure you’re running the latest version of iOS
  2. Restart the app
  3. Restart your device

If the issue persists, contact support using the email above.
