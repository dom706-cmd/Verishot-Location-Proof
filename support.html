# VeriShot Location Proof — Support Welcome to **VeriShot Location Proof** support. Below you’ll find answers to common questions and ways to get help. --- ## Contact Support If you need help, have questions, or want to report an issue: **Email:** verisnappapp@gmail.com (You may replace this with a dedicated Gmail, e.g. `verishot.support@gmail.com`) Please include: - Your device model - iOS version - A brief description of the issue --- ## Frequently Asked Questions ### What is VeriShot Location Proof? VeriShot Location Proof allows you to capture photos that include verification metadata such as timestamp and location coordinates. These proof records are stored on your device and can be viewed, shared, or managed by you. --- ### Where is my data stored? All photos and verification data are stored **locally on your device** unless you choose to share them using iOS sharing features. We do not upload your photos or proof data to our own servers. --- ### Why does the app request location access? Location access allows the app to attach latitude and longitude data to a photo at the moment it is captured, helping establish proof of where a photo was taken. You can enable or disable location access at any time in **iOS Settings**. --- ### How do subscriptions work? VeriShot offers optional subscriptions that unlock higher verification limits and additional features. - Payments are handled securely by Apple via the App Store. - Subscription status is managed through your Apple ID. - You can manage or cancel your subscription in **iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions**. --- ### How do I restore a previous purchase? If you’ve already subscribed and reinstalled the app or switched devices: 1. Open the app 2. Go to the **Upgrade** tab 3. Tap **Restore Purchases** --- ### What happens if I reach the free verification limit? Free users are limited to a set number of verifications per month. Once the limit is reached, you can upgrade to continue using the app without interruption. --- ### How do I delete a photo or proof record? You can delete proof records directly from within the app. Deleting the app from your device will also remove all locally stored data. --- ### The app is not working correctly. What should I do? Please try the following: 1. Make sure you’re running the latest version of iOS 2. Restart the app 3. Restart your device If the issue persists, contact support using the email above. --- ## Legal - [Privacy Policy](privacy) - Terms of Service available upon request --- ## App Information **App Name:** VeriShot Location Proof **Platform:** iOS **Developer:** Dominic Aleandri
